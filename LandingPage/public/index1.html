ABOUT US

We are a work team made up of 6 students from the Peruvian University of Applied Sciences, we
identified a common problem that exists in various cities around the world and it is the issue that
there are constantly lines of cars that want to park but have to wait for a space is available or find
another parking space. So, as software engineering students, we devised a solution for this
problem, which consists of an application that can report available nearby parking lots.

MISION

We want to solve the problem with the available places in the parking lots and for people who want to find a fast place and that is available we generate our application

VISION

Due to the fact that the current system of parking and available places is not effective since they generate queues of cars, we as a company decided to improve and in the future join these systems for a more constant update and the client can go to a free parking place, nearby, so fast
